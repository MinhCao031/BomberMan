TUTORIAL
1. FOR ONE PLAYER
* Use button "UP"  "DOWN","RIGHT","LEFT" to move your character up, down,right,left.
* Use button "Enter" to place bomb.
* If you have the "Bomb speed" item, use button "/" to activate the bomb.

2. FOR TWO PLAYERS.
* One person plays as described in the "1. FOR ONE PLAYER" part.
* The other person uses button "a","s","d","w" to move your character left,down,right,up.
* Use button "b" to place bomb.
* If you have the "Bomb speed" item, use button "z" to activate the bomb.

** REMINDER **
TRY TO FIND THE DOOR AS MUCH AS YOU CAN.

DO NOT TOUCH YOUR ENEMIES OR LET THE TIME RUN OUT.
